The Story Behind This Work

Amber Drift celebrates the beauty of dissolution—where solid forms slowly transform into fluid fields of color. Warm golden tones drift and merge, creating a sense of weightlessness and dreamy luminosity that invites contemplation of transformation.

The work explores how identity and form dissolve in moments of surrender. Rather than fighting the process, the composition embraces it, allowing warm ambiance to overwhelm the canvas, suggesting that in the letting go, something more essential emerges.
